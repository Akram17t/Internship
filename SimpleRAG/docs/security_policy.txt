INFORMATION SECURITY POLICY

Version: 3.0
Last Updated: March 2026

Purpose

This document defines the security requirements that all employees and contractors must follow.

Password Requirements

Passwords must contain at least 12 characters and include uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.

Multi-Factor Authentication

Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all company accounts, including email, cloud platforms, and internal applications.

Data Classification

Company data is classified into four categories:

1. Public
2. Internal
3. Confidential
4. Restricted

Confidential and Restricted data must not be shared outside the organization without authorization.

Access Control

Employees are granted access according to the principle of least privilege. Access requests must be approved by the relevant department manager.

Incident Reporting

Security incidents must be reported to the Information Security Team within one hour of discovery.

Remote Work

Employees working remotely must connect through the company VPN when accessing internal resources.

Device Management

Only company-approved devices may access production systems and confidential information.
