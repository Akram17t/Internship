LEAVE POLICY

Version: 2.1
Last Updated: January 2026

Purpose

This policy describes the leave entitlements available to all full-time employees of the company.

Annual Leave

All full-time employees are entitled to 18 days of paid annual leave per calendar year. Annual leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 7 days before the intended leave date.

Unused annual leave may be carried forward to the next calendar year, up to a maximum of 5 days. Any remaining unused leave above this limit will expire.

Sick Leave

Employees are entitled to 12 days of paid sick leave annually. A medical certificate may be required for absences longer than 2 consecutive working days.

Maternity Leave

Eligible employees are entitled to 90 days of maternity leave in accordance with company regulations.

Emergency Leave

Employees may request emergency leave for urgent family situations. Approval is subject to review by the employee's manager and HR department.

Approval Process

All leave requests require approval from the employee's direct manager. HR maintains the official leave balance records.
